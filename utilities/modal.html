If this question is chosen as the question-that-counts, you maximize your chance of receiving $${e://Field/bonus} by providing your most accurate guess (details).

When asked to guess the percent chance of some outcome being true, let's call your guess G. To determine whether you earn $1 from that guess, a computer will randomly select a number N from 1 to 100:

  • If that number is less than or equal to your guess, G, you will earn $1 if the outcome is true.
  • If that number, N, is greater than your guess, you will instead earn $1 with a N% chance.

This means that, to secure the largest chance of earning $1 from your guess, you should report your most accurate guess.
